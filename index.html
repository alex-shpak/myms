About Outfit

Myrmidons United (MyMs) is a English-speaking gaming community centered around Planetside 2. Primary faction of the outfit is Terran Republic (TR) on the server Miller, with focus on Combined Arms Tactical Gameplay. The community is run by the Administration whose current lineup is as follows:
- Leaders: kimpumomo, themadkosovan, frostbite
- Officers: walazor.
Our vision is to become a formidable, respected and efficient gaming community, able and capable of fighting against heavy odds, anywhere, anytime. The main mission of Myrmidons United (MyMs) is to build a friendly and relaxed community, based on positive values of trust, unity, cooperation and willingness to learn; creating a formidable, frightening and mercilessly effective player base, while staying level headed, respectful and open towards all parts of the community and passionate about the glorious game that is Planetside 2, for years to come.
